\section*{Preface}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Preface}

This bachelor thesis has been submitted at the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Technical University of Denmark in partial fulfilment of the requirements for acquiring a bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering at the Techincal University of Denmark.

The project was done with the Open Additive Manufacturing (Open AM) Group, Department of Mechanical Engineering, at the Technical University of Denmark, DTU.

David Bue Pedersen, Sebastian Aagard Andersen and Magnus Bolt Kjer have been supervising the work, which was carried out during the period 2022-03-10 to 2022-06-23. The work in the project was set to equal 17.5 ECTS.
